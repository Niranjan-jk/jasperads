Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our video editing services and process.

What's your typical turnaround time?

Our standard turnaround time is 7-10 business days for most projects. However, we offer expedited services (3-5 days) for an additional 25% fee. Complex projects or those requiring extensive revisions may take longer.

How many revisions do you include?

We include 2 rounds of revisions in our standard packages. Additional revisions can be purchased if needed. Our goal is to get it right the first time, so we work closely with you during the initial briefing phase.

What file formats do you deliver?

We deliver in all standard formats (MP4, MOV, etc.) optimized for your specific platform needs (YouTube, Instagram, etc.). We also provide project files (Premiere Pro, After Effects) upon request for an additional fee.

Do you offer ongoing video editing services?

Yes! Many of our clients opt for our monthly retainer packages which offer discounted rates and priority scheduling. These are ideal for brands with consistent video content needs.

What information do you need to start a project?

We'll need your raw footage, brand guidelines (colors, fonts, logos), any specific music preferences, and a detailed brief outlining your goals, target audience, and key messages. We provide a comprehensive questionnaire to gather all necessary information.
